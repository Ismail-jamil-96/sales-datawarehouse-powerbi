## 1. Business Context
The company operates in a retail environment with multiple product categories and customers across different countries.
Management requires better visibility into sales performance, customer behavior, product pricing, and seasonality to support data-driven decisions.

---

## 2. Business Problems Addressed
This project was designed to answer the following key questions:

### Sales & Performance
- How has total sales evolved over time?
- Is growth driven by higher sales volume or pricing?
- Which categories and product lines contribute most to revenue and profit?

### Customer Analysis
- What proportion of customers are repeat buyers?
- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- Which customer age groups contribute most to sales?

### Product & Pricing
- Which subcategories sell the highest quantities?
- Are there products with high prices but low demand?
- Which products show strong perceived value?

### Time & Seasonality
- Are there seasonal patterns in sales and quantity?
- When do sales peak or decline throughout the year?

---

## 3. Approach
An end-to-end BI solution was implemented using a layered data architecture:
- Bronze layer for raw ingestion
- Silver layer for cleansing and standardization
- Gold layer for analytical modeling using a star schema

Power BI was used to create interactive dashboards for business users.

---

## 4. Key Outcomes
- Identified revenue growth patterns and seasonal peaks
- Highlighted the importance of repeat customers
- Revealed pricing and demand imbalances at product level
- Provided profit-based insights beyond revenue alone

---

## 5. Limitations & Assumptions
- Analysis is based on historical data only
- Dataset is static and used for educational/portfolio purposes
- Profit calculations rely on available cost fields

---

## 6. Tools Used
SQL Server, Power BI, DAX, Power Query
